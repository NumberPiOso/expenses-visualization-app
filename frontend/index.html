EXPENSES VISUALIZATION PROJECT

I plan to create a expenses visualization web application for personal use. This should help me keep track of my incomes and expenses, helping me to take better decisions. I think I will do it using FastAPI and Postgresql at backend, besides that, may god help me.

Using this project you can

  • Keep tracked all your incomes and expenses
  • Identify your expenses by predefined categories
  • (Soon) Categorize expenses with custom tags

HOW TO USE IT?

  • Register a income or a expense
  • Visualize your expenses

    • You can create your own custom avatar for the masthead, change the icon in the dividers, and add your email address to the contact form to make it fully functional!
